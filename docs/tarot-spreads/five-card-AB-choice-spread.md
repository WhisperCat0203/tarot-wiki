# Five-Card A/B Choice Spread

- *Suitable for*: Choosing between two options, situations, or relationships (Option A vs. Option B)
- *Example questions*:  
  Should I choose Job A or Job B?  
  Should I reconcile with my past relationship or start a new one?  
  Which path is more suitable for me?

- *Card position meanings*:  
  1: Your current situation  
  2: The development of Option A  
  3: The development of Option B  
  4: The outcome and cost of choosing Option A  
  5: The outcome and cost of choosing Option B
