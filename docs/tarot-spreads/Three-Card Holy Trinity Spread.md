# Holy Trinity Spread

- *Suitable for*: Clarifying complex situations, revealing surface appearances versus hidden truths, and exploring mutual thoughts within a relationship
- *Example questions*:  
  What is the real blockage I am facing right now?  
  What aspects of this situation are only on the surface, and what have I been overlooking?  
  In my relationship with this person, how do I see them, where is the relationship heading, and how do they see me?

- *Card position meanings*:  
  1: Core issue / What you are truly facing / Development of the relationship  
  2: Surface situation / External influences / Your thoughts about them  
  3: Hidden factors / Subconscious influences / Their thoughts about you
