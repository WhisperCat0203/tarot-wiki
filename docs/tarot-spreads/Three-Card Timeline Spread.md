# Three-Card Timeline Spread

- *Suitable for*: Tracking the development of events or relationships / Exploring causes and outcomes
- *Example questions*:  
  What are the past, present, and future of this situation or person?  
  What is the issue, its cause, and its outcome?

- *Card position meanings*:  
  1: Past / Cause  
  2: Present / Current state  
  3: Future / Development trend
