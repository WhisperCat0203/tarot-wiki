# Four-Card Advice Spread

- *Suitable for*: Analyzing situations before making a decision, gaining clarity on self, environment, and outcome, and receiving guidance
- *Example questions*:  
  Should I continue with this matter?  
  Is this the right time to make this decision?  
  What advice will help me move forward successfully?  
  What are the key challenges and turning points in advancing this situation?

- *Card position meanings*:  
  1: Your current state  
  2: Obstacles / Challenges / External environment  
  3: Supportive forces / Strengths / Inner motivation  
  4: Outcome / Advice card
